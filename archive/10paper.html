Prioritizing Happiness: The Life-Changing Investments

  • By Andre V Silva
  • Nov 29, 2023
  • FinanceFinancial Education

The emphasis is on prioritizing what truly matters over material possessions. Here 9 Things I Purchased to Enhance My Life.

In a society where the chase of riches and worldly stuff is frequently the driving force, I've found consolation in refocusing my attention on things that truly matters. Despite a significant increase in income, my personal expenditure has reached an all-time low. The explanation is straightforward: if it does not add to my enjoyment, health, or financial goals, it is not included. In this essay, I'll go over nine individual investments that have had a significant influence on different elements of my life.

1. Journal/Planner: A Record of Progress

My trusty diary and planner was one of the first and most significant investments I made. I started journaling years ago, and it has had an enormous influence on my life. This multi-functional tool functions as both a self-analysis tour and a weekly planner. Writing down my experiences, thoughts, and ideas has helped me to become more conscious, focused, and deliberate in my activities.

2. A Literary Escape at the Midnight Library

The second investment that has had a huge impact on my viewpoint is a book called "The Midnight Library" by Matt Hague. Hague, a prolific novelist, created a masterwork that bridges the gap between fiction and self-help. The story follows Nora, a lady nearing the end of her life, as she explores various universes based on different life choices. It serves as a painful reminder that no life is flawless and highlights the benefits of living.

3. Black Top: Decision Simplifying for Consistency

My black shirt is a seemingly simple yet significant purchase. I adore it so much that I have two of them. Aside from being extremely comfy and warm, it has simplified my life, particularly while making YouTube videos. I've developed a no-brainer atmosphere that fosters consistency and efficiency by eliminating selections in many areas, such as wardrobe choices.

4. Outsourcing: Time and Energy Invested

While not a physical thing, outsourcing has become a key priority in my spending habits. I outsource duties like furniture assembly and cleaning because I am inspired by Naval Ravikant's concept on the value of time. the cost is less than my desired hourly rate, it's a no-brainer investment in recuperating valuable time, energy, and brainpower.

5. Fuel Essential Meal: Productivity Nourishing

Investing in Fuel's essential meal has been a game changer in terms of health and productivity. The 100% plant-based essential powder, especially the chocolate-flavored variety, guarantees that I satisfy my nutritional requirements without compromising time. It is quick and convenient, and it eliminates the need for meal-related decision-making and cleaning, allowing me to continue working without interruption.

6. Fast Tech: Efficient Income Stream Upgrades

Recognizing the necessity of effective instruments for income-generating activities, I make prudent investments in technological advancements. While I don't believe in constantly upgrading only to get the latest version, ensuring my laptop runs at optimal efficiency is a wise investment. Given the importance of my laptop to my revenue sources, spending money on superior technology typically results in additional money coming in.

7. Learning Investing: The Knowledge Currency

Learning became a priority for me after high school. While cultural pressures frequently favor grades above comprehension, investing in courses has expedited my learning curve. Despite reservations about online courses, investing in the proper ones has proven to be useful. In an age where knowledge is easily available, the correct course may expedite your learning and development.

8. Whiteboard: Goal Mastery Visualization

The whiteboard at my workspace is a visual assistance that has had a big influence on my goal-setting process. It acts as a continual reminder, with overall goals at the top and weekly actions below. The emphasis is on leading indicators, or daily behaviors that genuinely drive growth. Updating it every Sunday night improves accountability and gives a clear plan for the coming week.

9. Activities and Experiences: Investing in Memories

Finally, spending money on experiences and attempting new things has been a deliberate decision. Whether it's a weekend in the country or trekking into uncharted territory, these experiences help to form lasting memories. It serves as a reminder to not become so engrossed in the quest of financial success that I forget to live and create treasured experiences.

Finally, these nine investments cover a wide range of facets of my life, illustrating the necessity of prioritizing what genuinely important. These investments have together formed a more deliberate and satisfying existence, whether it's improving mindfulness, simplifying decisions, or supporting health and learning. The message is clear: the worth of an investment extends beyond its monetary value to the tremendous influence it has on one's quality of life.
